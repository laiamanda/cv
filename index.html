About Me

Graduated from University at Albany with a B.S in Computer Science. I focused my academic coursework’s on algorithms, data structure, and mathematics. I am particularly interested in Web Development, UX/UI, and Marketing. Initially when I started my college career, I felt uneasy in my major. I always wanted to learn programming, however, my coursework made me doubt myself and my skills. By my junior year, I decided to take a web development thinking nothing about it.

Since then, I have enjoyed web development and have been honing my skills by working on various side-projects. I even completed a Udemy’s course focusing on Full-Stack development back in 2020. Besides my enjoyment in web development, I enjoy the arts. I like spending my free time writing stories, drawing, painting, and playing instruments. When I am not working on any art projects or code, you can find me spending my time on working on a podcast with my friends, baking, or watching dramas and anime.

This year, my most important goal is to focus on my health and my family. Last year, I decided to focus my code on quantity rather than quality. This year, it's the opposite. This year, I plan to learn more programming languages and tools and show it on Github. I want to work on bigger projects that I'm interested in, draw more, and keep applying to jobs.
